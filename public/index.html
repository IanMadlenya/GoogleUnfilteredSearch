Unfiltered.Search

Unfiltered.Search allows you explore what's on the minds of others across the country. This app is inspired by Jigsaw's Unfiltered.News.

  • Basics: Each circle represents a state. Type a keyword to resize the states by how much its people search for that keyword (over the last 24 hours).
  • Positioning: Toggle between Geography and 2016 Election Outcome to position the states based on these factors.
  • Related queries: Searches related to your keyword will appear, with numbers indicating their relatedness.
